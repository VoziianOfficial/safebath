Information We Collect

SafeBath may collect information that homeowners choose to submit through forms, quote requests, email communications, or other direct contact methods available on the platform.

This may include information such as name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, selected service category, and project notes related to walk-in tub interests, accessibility needs, bathroom layout, comfort priorities, or similar request context.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through the platform may be used to support quote-request pathways, improve user experience, respond to inquiries, maintain platform functionality, and help structure homeowner-provider matching flows.

Information may also be used to improve page layout, content flow, and interaction quality across service categories, legal pages, and contact experiences.

Quote Requests

When homeowners submit quote requests, information may be used as part of the process that helps connect users with independent local service providers or companies that may serve the requested area.

SafeBath is an independent platform and is not a direct installer, remodeling contractor, or bathroom-service company.

Cookies and Similar Technology

The platform may use cookies and similar technologies to support essential functions, remember preference choices, improve navigation experience, and maintain more consistent site performance.

For more information, please review the Cookie Policy.

Information Sharing

Information may be shared where reasonably necessary to support platform operations, quote-request handling, legal compliance, service-provider connection flows, or related platform functions.

All contractors/providers remain independent, and the platform does not represent itself as directly performing the underlying work.

Data Security

Reasonable efforts may be used to help protect submitted information and maintain general site integrity. However, no website, platform, or transmission system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Users may choose whether to submit information through forms, whether to contact the platform by email or phone, and whether to accept or decline cookie-related preferences where that option is available.
